Books I've Read

This year I decided that I wanted to get into reading more. Generally, when I read a book I want to gain something from it. Whether that be knowledge about a topic or just plain recreation. I've read a variety of books so far, here a few of the recent ones.

1984

George Orwell

1984 takes place in an extremely totalitarian and dystopian society. The world building/setting is very cool. This book was a bit of a hard read, and at times quite heavy, but overall worth it.

★★★★☆


Immune

Phillip Dettmer

This book provides an excellent explanation about how the human immune system works. It is very well written, explaining complex and convoluted systems in a way that is easy to understand.

★★★★★
